Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a cloud migration take?

Most small business migrations (email, files, and basic applications) take 1-2 weeks of planning and a weekend for the actual cutover. Larger or more complex migrations with custom applications may take 4-8 weeks. We plan everything to ensure zero downtime during business hours.

Will we lose any data during migration?

No. We maintain complete backups of all data before, during, and after migration. Our process includes data verification at every step, and we keep your original systems running in parallel until the migration is fully validated. We have a 100% data migration success rate.

Is the cloud secure for our business data?

Yes‚Äîcloud platforms like Microsoft 365 and Azure invest billions in security, far exceeding what any small business could afford on-premise. We add additional layers including multi-factor authentication, conditional access policies, data loss prevention, and email encryption to keep your data secure.

What happens to our old servers after migration?

Once migration is complete and verified, we help you decommission old servers and properly wipe any remaining data. Many clients repurpose their server room space. Some businesses keep a minimal on-premise presence for specific applications‚Äîwe help you determine the best hybrid approach.

How much does cloud migration cost?

Cloud migration is typically a one-time project fee based on the complexity of your environment, plus ongoing monthly per-user costs for cloud services. Most businesses find their total monthly costs decrease 20-40% compared to maintaining on-premise servers when you factor in hardware, electricity, and maintenance savings.

Frequently Asked Questions

How often are backups taken?

We configure backups as frequently as every 15 minutes for critical systems, with full daily backups as a baseline. Backup frequency is customized to your business needs‚Äîthe goal is that you never lose more than a few minutes of work, even in a worst-case scenario.

How quickly can you restore our systems after a disaster?

For most scenarios, we can have your critical systems running again within an hour. The exact recovery time depends on the scope of the disaster and the amount of data involved. We test recovery quarterly so you know exactly what to expect‚Äîno guessing when it matters most.

Are backups protected from ransomware?

Yes. We maintain air-gapped and immutable backup copies that ransomware cannot reach or encrypt. Even if every computer in your office is compromised, your backup data remains clean and ready to restore. This is the single most important defense against ransomware.

Does Microsoft 365 back up our data automatically?

No‚Äîthis is a common misconception. Microsoft's retention policies are not a backup solution. If an employee accidentally deletes files, an account is compromised, or data is corrupted, Microsoft may not be able to recover it. We provide dedicated Microsoft 365 backup that protects your email, SharePoint, OneDrive, and Teams data.

How much does business continuity planning cost?

Pricing depends on the amount of data you need to protect, how frequently you need backups, and your recovery time requirements. Most small businesses invest a fraction of what a single data loss incident would cost. We'll provide exact pricing during your free backup assessment.

How fast do you actually respond to emergencies?

Our guarantee is 15 minutes for emergency issues, and our actual average is 11 minutes. For remote issues, we're typically connected and working within 5-8 minutes. If onsite support is needed, we're never more than 30 minutes away anywhere in Northern Kentucky.

Do I need to be a managed IT client to get emergency support?

No‚Äîwe offer emergency support to any business in Northern Kentucky or Greater Cincinnati. However, our managed IT clients get priority response, and emergency support is included in their plan at no extra charge. Non-managed clients are billed at our standard hourly rate.

What counts as an IT emergency?

Any issue that prevents your business from operating normally: servers down, network outages, security breaches, email not working, critical data loss, or major hardware failures. If your team can't work because of a technology problem, that's an emergency and we treat it with urgency.

Is there an extra charge for after-hours or weekend emergencies?

For managed IT clients, emergency support is the same rate 24/7/365‚Äîno surprise overtime charges. For non-managed clients, we offer transparent hourly pricing that we'll confirm before starting work, so there are never surprise bills.

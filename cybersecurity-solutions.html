Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know if my business needs cybersecurity services?

If your business uses email, stores customer data, or connects to the internet, you need cybersecurity. Small and mid-sized businesses are actually the most targeted‚Äî43% of cyberattacks target small businesses. We offer a free security assessment to identify your specific vulnerabilities and risks.

What does a cybersecurity assessment include?

Our assessment includes vulnerability scanning of your network and devices, review of your current security policies and configurations, dark web scanning for compromised credentials, phishing susceptibility testing, and a detailed report with prioritized recommendations. The assessment is free and comes with no obligation.

How do you protect against ransomware?

We use a multi-layered approach: advanced endpoint protection that detects and blocks ransomware before it executes, email filtering to catch phishing attempts, employee security awareness training, network segmentation to limit spread, and automated backups with air-gapped copies so you can recover quickly if an attack gets through.

Do you help with HIPAA and compliance requirements?

Yes. We help Northern Kentucky businesses meet HIPAA, PCI-DSS, and other compliance requirements. This includes risk assessments, policy development, technical controls implementation, employee training, and ongoing monitoring. We provide documentation you need for audits and can serve as your virtual CISO.

What happens if we actually get breached?

We have an incident response plan ready to execute immediately. We contain the threat, assess the damage, preserve evidence, begin recovery, and help you meet notification requirements. Our 15-minute response guarantee applies to security incidents too. We also help with insurance claims and post-incident hardening.
